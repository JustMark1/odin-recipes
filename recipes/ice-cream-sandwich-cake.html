Ice Cream Sandwich Cake

The Recipe:

This ice cream sandwich cake was renamed as "The Best Dessert I've Ever Eaten" by my daughter this Memorial Day.

I had made it before, but my husband ate all of it before she had a chance to get a taste!
My husband doesn't like when I take this desert to a large gathering because it means he won't have any leftovers!

Recipe by DAYMOMMY

Prep Time: 30 mins
Additional Time: 30 mins
Total Time: 1 hrs

Ingredients

  • 24 vanilla ice cream sandwiches, unwrapped
  • 2 (8 ounce) containers whipped topping (such as Cool Whip®), thawed
  • 1 (12 ounce) jar hot fudge ice cream topping, warmed
  • 1 (12 ounce) jar caramel ice cream topping
  • ¼ cup chopped pecans, or to taste

Steps

  1. Arrange a layer of ice cream sandwiches in the bottom of a 9x13-inch dish; top with a layer of whipped topping, hot fudge topping, and caramel topping.
    Repeat layering with remaining ice cream sandwiches, whipped topping, hot fudge topping, and caramel topping, ending with a top layer of whipped topping. Sprinkle with pecans.
  2. Cover the dish with aluminum foil and freeze until set, about 30 minutes.

Enjoy!
